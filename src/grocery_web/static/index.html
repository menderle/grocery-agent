Spend limits always apply — orders over your caps are blocked in every mode.

Update my payment card

Your prepaid card is saved on your H-E-B account. Change it on heb.com or in the H-E-B app — the agent always uses your account's default card. (The web chat can't change payment methods, by design.)

Change my store

Set your curbside / home store in the H-E-B app or on heb.com. The agent orders from your account's store.

Save a “usual” item

Just tell the agent, e.g. “remember my usual water is the H-E-B 1877 12-pack.” It reuses it next time — here and in Claude.

Change spend limits

Per-order, weekly, and monthly caps always block over-limit orders. Adjust them via the Claude connector (“set my weekly limit to $250”) or in config/policy.yaml on the host.

Turn on real ordering

The app runs in Test mode (dry-run) — it rehearses checkout without charging. To place real orders, disable test mode on the host (HEB_CHECKOUT_DRY_RUN=false, then restart).

“H-E-B signed out” or no prices

Your H-E-B login lives in a browser on the host. If searches return no prices or it shows “signed out,” re-open the parked Chrome and sign in to heb.com again.
